SUEST EXAMPLE 6.5 EXACT-REPLICATION FIX

The Example 6.5 discrepancy was not caused by sample size. It was caused by
the estimand:

- The Stata example uses start(age=20), amount(10), and covariates(atmeans).
- The previous R page averaged a +10 change from each observed age over the
  observed covariate distribution.
- Since age enters quadratically, those are different effects.
- marginaleffects newdata = "mean" is also not exact here because it uses
  modes for factor variables, whereas Stata atmeans uses means of factor
  indicator columns.

This repository now supplies and uses the exact model-matrix atmeans helper
validated by the acceptance suite. It also adds sample-size checks to every
paper example.
