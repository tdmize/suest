# Seemingly Unrelated Estimation for Model Comparisons

The suest package combines two separately fitted regression models with
a joint model-robust covariance matrix and integrates with
marginaleffects for cross-model comparisons of predictions and effects.

## References

Mize, T. D., Doan, L., and Long, J. S. (2019). A General Framework for
Comparing Predictions and Marginal Effects Across Models. *Sociological
Methodology*, 49(1), 152–189. doi:10.1177/0081175019852763.
